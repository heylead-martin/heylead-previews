Digital Locks

Union Locksmiths have experience working on digital locks.

We have repair and replacement experience with lock models from various brands. These include, but are not limited to:

  • Samsung
  • Yale
  • Assa Abloy
  • Aqara
  • Genesys
  • iRevo Gateman
  • Kaiser Plus
  • Kaadas
  • Schlage

Please call or WhatsApp us at 8334-7296 if you would like our professionals to come sort out your digital lock issue.

Send us a video clip or pictures by WhatsApp showing the issue, then we will guide you from there on.

NOTE: We are an independent, professional locksmith service with the ability to break/remove, supply, install and service digital locks of different brands.

We are not a service centre for any digital lock brand, nor do we claim to have any such affiliation with any lock brands.

Our ability to repair any lock is influenced by the nature of the fault or issue in question, and our ability to take on the job is also limited by availability of spare parts for selected brands and models.
