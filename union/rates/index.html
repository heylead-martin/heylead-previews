Our Rates

Union means excellent work, quality products and great customer service - at fair, market rates.

Our wide range of products and materials from tried and tested manufacturers are carefully sourced from reliable suppliers.

As each job is unique, with specific requirements, our prices vary and depend on the complexity of the job.

Nevertheless, we understand customers need to set aside a budget and want the reassurance of knowing our prices before engaging us.

So here's a list of standard rates charged by professional locksmiths.

For a more accurate quote, give us a call and tell us your issue. We will advise accordingly.
