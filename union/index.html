Welcome to Union Locksmiths

We are professional locksmiths who provide a wide range of emergency access and security services for businesses and individuals, whether they are offices, factories and vehicles.

We help you quickly if your staff and customers are locked in or locked out of your premises or vehicle. We also fit new or replacement locks and repair your damaged or faulty locks.

We are different from other locksmiths because we:

  • Speak fluent English, basic Mandarin and Bahasa
  • Accept credit cards
  • Provide invoices / receipts
  • Are fully insured
  • Are Accredited and Registered
  • Are 100% Singaporean-owned and operated
  • Are independent of any lock brand

*We are not a service centre for any lock brand, nor do we claim to have any such affiliation.
