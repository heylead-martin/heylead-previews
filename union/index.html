Welcome to Union Locksmiths

We are professional locksmiths who provide a wide range of emergency access and security services for businesses, whether commercial, retail or industrial.

We work on locks, whether they are in offices, shops, factories, stores or vehicles of all kinds.

Call our Hotline 8334-7296.

We can help you quickly - often within 30-40min - if your staff and customers are locked in or locked out of your premises or vehicle. We also fit new or replacement locks and repair your damaged or faulty locks.

We are different from other locksmiths because we are:

  • able to speak fluent English and basic Mandarin
  • able to accept most credit cards
  • able to provide invoices or receipts
  • fully insured
  • accredited and registered
  • 100% Singaporean-owned and operated
  • independent of any lock brand*

*We are not a service centre for any lock brand, nor do we claim to have any such affiliation.
